Unitree Robotics

Unitree B2-W

Quadruped Robot

Unitree B2‑W is a rugged, wheeled-quadruped robot capable of 20 km/h all-terrain mobility, heavy payloads, and autonomous navigation.

Availability & Pricing

Price: ~$100,000 USD

Type: Industrial/Commercial

Status: Available for Order

Overview

The Unitree B2‑W is a hybrid quadruped robot from Unitree Robotics, combining legged agility with wheeled speed. Designed as an industrial/field-grade platform, it adapts to stairs, slopes, rough ground, and flat surfaces with seamless leg-wheel transformation. It's tailored for robust tasks such as inspection, logistics, rescue, and autonomous patrolling.

This innovative robot represents a breakthrough in adaptive locomotion technology, featuring the ability to switch between wheels and legs for optimal performance across various terrains. With its rugged IP67-rated design, the B2-W can operate in challenging environments while maintaining exceptional mobility and payload capacity.

The B2-W's advanced sensor suite includes 3D LiDAR, depth cameras, and optical cameras, enabling sophisticated autonomous navigation and mapping capabilities. Its high-capacity battery system provides extended operation times, making it ideal for long-duration missions in remote or hazardous locations.

Capabilities

Adaptive Locomotion: Seamlessly switches between wheels and legs for optimal performance across different terrains.
Terrain Resilience: Excellent balance on gravel, grass, stairs, inclines, and uneven ground with advanced stabilization.
Obstacle Navigation: Capable of stair navigation and acrobatic maneuvers like handstands and flares.
Autonomous Operations: Equipped with mapping, path planning, OTA updates, and remote control capabilities.
Heavy Lifting: Can carry over 40 kg while moving and support up to 120 kg statically for material transport.
Environmental Durability: IP67 protection enables operation in harsh weather and dusty conditions.

Applications

Industrial Inspection: Patrol pipelines, power grids, and remote infrastructures with autonomous navigation.
Logistics & Transport: Deliver goods across warehouses, farms, mines, or outdoor facilities with heavy payload capacity.
Emergency & Rescue: Navigate disaster zones, rubble, or steep terrain to support firefighting or rescue teams.
Research & Development: Versatile testbed for payload, sensor integrations, and autonomous navigation research.
Security & Surveillance: Autonomous site monitoring with onboard LiDAR and cameras for perimeter security.
Agricultural Operations: Field monitoring, crop inspection, and automated farming tasks in challenging terrain.
