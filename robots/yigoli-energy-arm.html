YIGOLI

Energy Arm

Industrial Automation Robot

Robotic arm that automates vehicle refueling using advanced 3D imaging and AI for precise fuel cap handling.

Overview

The YIGOLI Energy Arm is an innovative robotic system designed to automate the vehicle refueling process. Utilizing 3D stereoscopic imaging and laser radar, it accurately identifies and opens fuel caps, inserts the nozzle, and refuels vehicles without human intervention.

This cutting-edge technology enhances efficiency and safety at fueling stations while providing a seamless experience for drivers. The system represents a significant advancement in automotive service automation, combining precision robotics with intelligent recognition systems to revolutionize traditional gas station operations.

Capabilities

Automated Fuel Cap Recognition: Uses 3D imaging to identify and locate fuel caps on various vehicle models with high precision.
Precise Robotic Manipulation: Accurately opens fuel caps and inserts nozzles with mechanical precision and safety.
Intelligent Navigation: Advanced AI guidance system for optimal positioning and movement around vehicles.
Safety Monitoring: Continuous monitoring of the refueling process with automatic error detection and emergency stops.
Vehicle Compatibility: Adapts to different vehicle heights, fuel cap positions, and access requirements automatically.

Applications

Fueling Stations: Automating the refueling process for enhanced efficiency and reduced wait times at gas stations.
Fleet Management: Streamlining refueling operations for large vehicle fleets and logistics companies.
Highway Service Centers: Providing automated fueling services for long-distance travelers and commercial traffic.
Commercial Depots: Automated refueling for delivery trucks, buses, and commercial vehicles in fleet operations.
Smart Cities: Integration into intelligent transportation infrastructure for autonomous vehicle support systems.
