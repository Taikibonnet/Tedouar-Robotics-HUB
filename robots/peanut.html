Peanut Robotics

Peanut

Commercial Cleaning Robot

AI-powered cleaning robot designed for commercial spaces, operating autonomously with advanced navigation and disinfection capabilities.

Overview

Peanut is an advanced cleaning robot developed by Peanut Robotics, aimed at automating cleaning tasks in commercial environments such as hotels, offices, and stores. Utilizing transformer-based AI technology, Peanut navigates spaces independently, reaching difficult areas and operating continuously, except during charging periods.

Its autonomous operation enhances efficiency and reduces the need for human intervention in routine cleaning tasks. The robot combines sophisticated navigation algorithms with medical-grade disinfection capabilities, making it an ideal solution for maintaining hygiene standards in commercial spaces while optimizing operational costs.

Navigation

Capabilities

Autonomous Cleaning: Performs independent cleaning in various commercial settings without human supervision.
Advanced Navigation: Reaches tight and difficult areas using sophisticated AI pathfinding algorithms.
Continuous Learning: Enhances cleaning processes through ongoing data collection and analysis.
Medical-grade Disinfection: Provides EPA-rated disinfection with 99.999% efficacy against human coronaviruses.
Adaptive Operation: Adjusts cleaning patterns based on space layout and usage patterns.

Applications

Hospitality: Hotels and resorts for automated room and common area cleaning with consistent quality standards.
Corporate: Office buildings for routine maintenance and sanitization, improving workplace hygiene.
Retail: Commercial outlets for floor cleaning and surface disinfection during and after business hours.
Healthcare: Medical facilities for specialized cleaning and disinfection protocols to maintain sterile environments.
Education: Schools and universities for maintaining clean learning environments and reducing disease transmission.
